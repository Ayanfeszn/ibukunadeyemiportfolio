Oystack Launch

ClientOystack
RoleMarketing Video, Animation
Year2025
ToolsAfter Effects, Illustrator

Overview

I led the 2D motion graphics for Oystack's social launch — an AI research intelligence platform helping students, academics, and research teams discover, verify, and write with more confidence.

The challenge was translating a genuinely dense product — AI-assisted discovery, citation, writing — into something that reads instantly on a phone screen. I broke the platform's value down into three clear, bite-sized moments: research smarter, verify faster, publish with confidence.
