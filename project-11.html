Brassmoney Virtual Card

ClientBrass
Role3D Product Animation
Year2025
ToolsCinema 4D, Redshift

Overview

A 3D product animation for Brassmoney's virtual card — part of Brass's business banking platform that lets Nigerian businesses issue unlimited virtual and physical cards to their team, set spend limits per card, and track exactly how every card holder spends.

The goal was to give a card that only exists on-screen the same tactile, premium presence as the physical corporate card, so it read as trustworthy and real even without ever touching a hand — clean render, confident material, and light that made the interface feel solid rather than abstract.
