Brass Corporate Card Launch

ClientBrass
RoleProduct Launch, 3D Animation
Year2025
ToolsCinema 4D, After Effects

Overview

3D product animation for the launch of Brass's corporate card, designed to give the physical card a hero moment across launch content and social.

The focus was on material and light — getting the card's finish, weight, and detailing to read convincingly in 3D, then building camera moves that felt premium without slowing the pacing down.
