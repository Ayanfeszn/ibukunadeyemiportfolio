Vervelife 2025

ClientInterswitch
RoleFitness Event Screen Content, Motion Design
Year2025
ToolsAfter Effects, Premiere Pro

The Brief

Vervelife is Interswitch's fitness event, and the 2025 edition needed a full set of on-screen assets to run it — content built for the pace and energy of a live workout session rather than a standard corporate stage.

That meant covering a specific list: trainer intro videos to bring energy before each session, holding screens to fill the room during downtime, name plates and session plates to keep people oriented, and a logo animation to open and brand the event on screen.

Concept & Direction

The direction leaned into Vervelife's fitness energy — bold, high-tempo motion that matched a workout crowd rather than a conference room. At the same time, every asset needed to share one system, so a trainer's intro, a session plate, and a holding screen all felt like pieces of the same event rather than separate one-offs.
