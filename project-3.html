Catlog Brand Illustrations

ClientCatlog
Role3D Illustration, App & Social Design
Year2025
ToolsCinema 4D, Redshift

Overview

A full library of 3D icon illustrations for Catlog, the business management app that helps African merchants run an online store, take payments, and manage records without the chaos of doing it all by hand.

Every action in the product — checkout, coupons, receipts, invoices, deliveries, payouts, KYC, security — got its own small 3D object, designed as one consistent set so the app, social posts, and video content could all pull from the same visual language instead of feeling like separate design efforts.

Process-wise, most of the work happened in the lighting and material setup in Cinema 4D — building a single rig and shading system in Redshift that every icon could be dropped into, so lighting stayed consistent across the whole set. Materials were tuned carefully to match Catlog's brand tone, keeping the same warmth and finish whether an object was matte, glossy, or metallic.
