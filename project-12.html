Quickteller Digital Campaign

ClientInterswitch
Role2D Animation
Year2025
ToolsAfter Effects, Illustrator

Overview

I led the 2D animation campaign for Quickteller, Interswitch's consumer payments platform for airtime, bill payments, transfers, and eCash — used daily by millions of Nigerians to pay for everyday things without a bank card in hand.

My job was making mundane, everyday actions — paying a light bill, topping up airtime, sending money to a friend — feel quick and satisfying on screen. I carried the message across digital and social with simple character and icon animation, matched to the pace of a platform built around speed.
