Catlog Social Media Videos

ClientCatlog
RoleSocial Media Video, 2D Animation
Year2025
ToolsAfter Effects, Illustrator

Overview

I produced a series of 2D motion graphics videos for Catlog's social channels, keeping the brand's feed feeling alive and consistent in between bigger launches.

Each piece is built as a short, scroll-stopping loop, pulling directly from Catlog's illustration style and color language — so the social content and the brand identity read as one continuous system, not two separate jobs.
