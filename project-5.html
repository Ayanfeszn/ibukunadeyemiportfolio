Grip Brand Identity

ClientGrip
RoleGraphic Design, Motion & Illustration
Year2025
ToolsIllustrator, After Effects, Cinema 4D

Overview

A full brand identity for Grip, spanning graphic design, motion, and illustration — built to give the brand a consistent voice across static and moving assets alike.

Rather than treating identity and motion as separate phases, the system was designed to move from the start: logo behavior, illustration style, and animation principles were developed together so nothing needed retrofitting later.
