Run It Lyric Video

ClientQuickteller (Interswitch)
Role2D Lyric Video Animation
Year2025
ToolsAfter Effects, Illustrator

Overview

A lyric-style animated video made for "Run It," a campaign track produced for Quickteller — built to give the campaign's jingle a visual life of its own across social and digital placements.

The animation leaned on bold kinetic type and simple character motion, timed tightly to the track's rhythm so the words themselves became part of the choreography — energetic, fast-paced, and built to hold attention in a short scroll-stopping format.
