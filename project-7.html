Overview

Ayanfe Haven is a brand I built end to end — I designed, built, and shipped the website myself, and everything a visitor sees and clicks lives inside a system I own: logo, color story, packaging, creative direction, and the photography that carries it all.

The brand sells art-covered stationery, candles, and lifestyle objects, curated into gift boxes built to order. I built the identity from the mark up — warm, personal, unmistakably considered — then carried that same voice through packaging, product photography, and the site itself, so nothing about the experience feels bolted on.

ayanfehaven.com

Website Design & Build

I designed and built ayanfehaven.com from the ground up — shop, curated gift-box builder, artist features, and brand storytelling, all living on one site with a consistent visual language. Every section, from the homepage hero to the category pages, carries the same warm, editorial feel, so browsing the store reads like flipping through a keepsake rather than a checkout flow.

Brand Identity

I built the identity from a single wordmark — a soft, hand-lettered mark paired with a warm, saturated palette — then carried it across every surface the brand touches, from the site to the packaging to the tissue paper inside the box.

Packaging Design

Every box, mailer, and insert runs on the same system — one wordmark, one palette, one tone of voice. I designed the packaging to do double duty: protect the product, and make the unboxing feel like part of the gift.

Packaging & Product Photography

I shot the product and packaging photography myself — flat lays, held-in-hand lifestyle shots, and real unboxing moments — so the brand's warmth comes through in how the product actually looks and feels, not just how it's described.

I led creative direction and brand management across every touchpoint — the site, the packaging, the photography — keeping one voice consistent as the brand grew from a single product line into a full lifestyle catalog.

Outcome

Ayanfe Haven now runs as a complete brand experience I built and still steer — a live storefront, a packaging system that holds its own as a gift, and a visual identity that reads the same whether you're looking at the site, the box, or the product inside it.
